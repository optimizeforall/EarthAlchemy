My Story

Earth Tea came about over 4 years of careful experimentation. I started with Moringa leaf when I discovered I wasn't getting all the nutrients I needed from my diet, and my blood sugar levels were inconsistent. Moringa quickly became my solution - not only did it help regulate my blood sugar, but it also provided the essential nutrients I was missing. I found it improved my digestion and energy levels as well. I began slowly by adding other ingredients such as Ginger and Ceylon Cinnamon, choosing Ceylon specifically for its lower coumarin content compared to regular cinnamon, making it a healthier choice for daily consumption.

I aimed to create the healthiest blend possible, discovering that many ingredients synergized beautifully with Moringa, enhancing its effects. Ginger amplified the digestive benefits, cloves contributed additional anti-inflammatory properties, cayenne improved circulation and metabolism, while lavender harmoniously balanced the blend with its smooth, calming notes.

Earth tea is my daily companion - I drink it mornings, evenings, and after large meals. It helps me stay grounded and energized throughout the day. It's a gift given to me by Mother Earth, and I am deeply grateful to share it with you.
